How it started

When I was very young, every time I was asked "What do you want to be when you grow up?" I always had a different answer. At first I wanted to be a doctor, a singer, an astronaut, a librarian, a painter... When I grew up I said I wanted to be a psychologist, a teacher, a field journalist, a photographer and so on. Each job had some relevance to me, but I didn't know exactly what I needed to do to get there. Or if the choices were really right for me.

Even as an adult, sometimes it's hard. You may have some ideas about what you want to do, but even so, there are a lot of causes that can influence the choices you can make. This app can't tell you what you need to do next, but it will help in providing with all the necessary information and guide you to the most suitable path.

Key features

  1. Personality provider - Discover your unique strengths, preferences, and personality traits through our comprehensive personality test.
  2. Diverse job profiles - Each profile provides in-depth information, including job responsibilities, required skills, salary ranges, and growth prospects.
  3. Personal assistent - Our innovative chatbot will guide every step you make. It will be your personal assistent in your career development.
  4. Latest news - Stay updated with the latest industry trends and opportunities by accessing a calendar of career events, webinars, and workshops.
  5. Career progress tracker - Monitor your progress towards your career goals. Track completed courses, certifications, and interesting jobs to stay motivated and organized.
  6. Networking - Gain inspiration from real-life success stories and testimonials shared by professionals who have successfully found their dream careers.
